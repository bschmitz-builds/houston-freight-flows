Notes & sources

Port-attributable = FAF import/export flows entering/leaving by water at the Houston FAF zone — the seaport complex dominates, but the zone gateway also includes IAH air cargo.

Trucks are modeled flows (FAF 5.7.1, 2024) routed on the FAF5 network, anchored to USACE WCSC port tonnage. Ships are real AIS transits (MarineCadastre). One loop ≈ the longest truck route; particle counts are proportional to tonnage within each flow type (never across types).

Sources: BTS/FHWA FAF5 · USACE WCSC · TxDOT Roadway Inventory · NOAA MarineCadastre AIS.
